About Cyber Sentinels

Welcome to Cyber Sentinels 2023, the most anticipated gathering of the year! Join us in November for a week filled with excitement, talent, and camaraderie.


Our event features a diverse range of activities:


Tech Enthusiasts


  • Hackathon - A competitive coding challenge that tests your programming skills.

  • CTF (Capture The Flag) - A cybersecurity competition that pits your skills against challenging puzzles.

  • Ideathon - A brainstorming session to ideate and solve real-world problems.

Gaming Enthusiasts


  • Valorant Tournament - Showcase your tactical skills in this intense gaming competition.

  • BGMI Championship - Battle your way to victory in the BGMI Championship.


Entertainment


Concert Night - Join us for a night of music, dance, and fun with top artists. Get ready to dance the night away!

Mark your calendars for an unforgettable experience!
